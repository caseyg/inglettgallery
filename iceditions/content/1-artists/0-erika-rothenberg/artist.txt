Text: 

Title: Greetings

Medium: Screenprint on Arches Cover

Size: 8 1/2 x 6 in.

Edition: Five images in portfolio; Edition of 125, 20 Artist Proofs, 4 Printers Proofs, 1 I.C.E. Impression

Release: April 1993

Price: $ 600.00

ERIKA ROTHENBERG, GREETINGS, a boxed set of five "all-occasion" cards screenprinted by Robert Blanton at Brand X Editions, New York in an edition of 125, published by I.C. Editions, a division of Susan Inglett Gallery. Each card folds to 8.5" by 6" and is printed in up to 16 colors. Images in this portfolio represent a wide range of the artist's socio-political and cultural observations. The project can be maintained as a multiple within the greeting card box or as prints in an installation.

GREETINGS can be found in the collections of the Art Institute of Chicago; Allen Memorial Art Museum at Oberlin College; Getty Center Library, Santa Monica; Jewish Museum, San Francisco; Milwaukee Art Museum; Museum of Women in the Arts, Washington, D.C.; New School for Social Research, NYC; New York Public Library, NYC; Toledo Museum of Art; University of California, Los Angeles; Virginia Commonwealth University, Richmond; and the Whitney Museum of American Art, NYC.
