Text: 

Title: You're Right

Medium: Lithograph.

Size: 9 x 24 in.

Edition: Edition of 200.

Release: November 2010

Price: $4,000.00

Barbara Kruger, American conceptual artist and founding member of the Pictures Generation, continues to challenge our assumptions by pairing images and texts in provocative and enlightening ways. Here Kruger once again exhibits her keen powers of observation for who would quibble with this indisputable insight?
